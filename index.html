Le Maylis

The Gîte Le Maylis, a first-category rural gîte, is located 25 km from Tangier in the direction of Asilah. Nestled on the southern slope of Jebel Zhirou, it offers magnificent views of the Rif foothills. Therese welcomes you to this lush setting, in the heart of the countryside, where tranquility and comfort ensure a restful stay. Built on a 5000 m² plot, the gîte has 6 double rooms and can accommodate up to 12 guests.

Activities

Surrounded by stunning landscapes, the gîte offers easy access to a variety of activities. From quad biking and horseback riding to relaxing beach outings, there’s something for every adventurer. Discover the natural beauty of northern Morocco and make unforgettable memories.

Horse Riding along the Beach and Forest

Near the gîte, you can enjoy horseback rides organized by the Royal Equestrian Club of Tangier. Whether along the beach, with the ocean breeze and waves in the background, or through the peaceful forest trails, this experience promises unforgettable moments. It’s a perfect way to explore nature while enjoying the relaxing rhythm of horseback riding.

Explore the Region on a Quad and watch the Sunset

The gîte is located in an ecotourism area in northern Morocco, offering diverse landscapes and a unique quad adventure. With Tanger Quad, ride along trails and dunes to discover the stunning scenery around Tangier. Adventure seekers will love this activity, which takes you deep into nature and allows you to see places otherwise inaccessible.

Relax and Unwind at the Beautiful Beaches

Located just 8 km from the coast, the gîte provides easy access to the splendid beaches of the Atlantic Ocean, ideal for swimming, diving, or simply taking a peaceful walk along the shore. Sidi Kacem Beach is perfect for those wanting to try diving in a beautiful setting, while Cape Spartel and the Hercules Caves invite you to explore the natural wonders along the coastline.

Guest Table

At Le Maylis, we offer our guests an exclusive dining service. Upon request, we prepare delicious meals featuring locally sourced ingredients that showcase the rich flavors of Moroccan and Mediterranean cuisine. Whether you’re looking to explore traditional Moroccan dishes or savor Mediterranean specialties, we ensure an authentic and memorable dining experience tailored to your tastes.
